Why people stay longer than they planned
"This isn't dinner. This is escapism."

La Cantina is where suburban Port Elizabeth comes to forget the week. Whether it's a date night, a birthday, a random Tuesday, or a live music session that pulled you in from the parking lot — once you're here, you stay.

The colours are loud. The tequila is cold. The tacos are real. The music hits different at night. And the crowd? They come back every week. That says everything.
